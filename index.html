  • Welcome To Healthy Office +

    People find it difficult to sit for prolong hours in the same place, we saw this issue mainly between office based employees and wanted to come up with a product which could solve this, in fact everyone knows the harmful consequences of sitting for long hours on office chair. Obesity and Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS) are the primarily caused by longer hours of working on computer and it is unavoidable in today's working culture. It is worth questioning that we are leading a path of evolution or devolution.

  • Beautiful An IOT based solution

    This project is based on a smart IOT product which is used to monitor the sitting pattern of office based employees. In order to detect the presence of the employees at the desk area, two sensors are permanently fixed beneath the work table of the employee. The values generated from these two sensors are logically "and" operated to activate the trigger pin, use of triggers along with mail and SMS integration, as soon as the pin is triggered an email is sent and once the sitting pattern of the user is detected it sends notification to the android based application.

  • Solution for Sedentary Behavior

    Implementation of the blink IOT device which is used to remind the user to take breaks from looking at the screen unceasingly. The main reason to develop these products is to achieve the goal of taking breaks from the office chair for at least 20 to 25 times a day.

What is Healthy Office?

A solution for Sedentary Behavior of the Employees.

  • Detects Sitting Position

    Device detects how long the employee sits in the same position and reminds him/her to take regular breaks. Sensors are positioned under the workstation to detect the sitting position of the employee. Triple Tracking feature will improve the reliability of the information. Other than the sensors, Trackmouse Event API and Windows Cognitive Service APIs are used.

  • SMS, Emails or Notifications

    Notifications will be sent to the user to take a break. A funny quote or shopping offer nearby will be sent to user without sending a usual boring notifications to take breaks. It will effect the user psychologically.

  • Game for you

    Unlike other usual healthy applications, we made your life into a virtual character. By detecting your regular breaks and your facial expressions, we created a game to motivate you to become more healthier during office hours.

How It Works

Two ultra sonic sensors will detect your sitting position
