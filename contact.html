Healthy Office

A sedentary behavior controller. People find it difficult to sit for prolong hours in the same place, we saw this issue mainly between office based employees and wanted to come up with a product which could solve this, in fact everyone knows the harmful consequences of sitting for long hours on office chair. Obesity and Computer Vision Syndrome (CVS) are the primarily caused by longer hours of working on computer and it is unavoidable in today's working culture. It is worth questioning that we are leading a path of evolution or devolution.
